About this song:

"Retry Now" (Japanese: いますぐ輪廻, Ima Sugu Rinne) is a dark, yandere-themed Vocaloid song by producer NAKISO featuring Hatsune Miku. It explores an endless, murderous cycle of reincarnation where a magical girl repeatedly kills her crush and herself to reset the timeline and find a perfect love story.

The Lore & Story:

The music video and lyrics are set up like a horror visual novel.
            
The Cycle: Miku acts as a magical girl who is hopelessly devoted to her crush/the viewer.
            
The Resets: Whenever their relationship fails or a timeline goes wrong, she murders her crush and takes her own life to "retry" in a new reincarnation.
            
Metamorphosis: She uses her magic to remember past timelines, changing her outfits and weapons with every rebirth in her obsessive search for the flawless ending.

Meaning & Themes:

The Japanese title Ima Sugu Rinne translates to "Reincarnate Right Now". It leans heavily into Buddhist concepts of Samsara (the cycle of birth, death, and rebirth). The song serves as a terrifying bait-and-switch; it sounds upbeat and poppy, but masks a tragic, obsessive pursuit where she throws away morality and sanity to secure a utopian romance.
